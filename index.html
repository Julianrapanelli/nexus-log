Who we are

My name is Ignacio Giordano, I'm the co-founder of Nexus Logistics Solutions.

I decided to set up this company after working more than 13 years in the Shipping Industry, not only as a Port Agent/Operator in all Argertina ports for bulk and container cargos, but also as part of a USA Freight Forwarder.

We offer several services, from agency services to small time provisions. All of them will be executed in a neat, personalized way, including full supervisión of all the services that are not carried out directly by us.

Experience has given us a wide knowledge and insight concerning what a vessel needs during its call, while minimizing expenses as much as possible and I would like an opportunity to show this on any imminent call to upriver ports.

We have a wide network that allows us to provide a cost-efficient service, which we believe is our most valuable asset.
