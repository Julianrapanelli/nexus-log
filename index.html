What we do

These are the services we bring. For any other service or special requirement, get in touch with us.

Spare parts: repair and purchase

To keep everything on the move, we have specialized technicians, workshops and suppliers to meet your needs. We know how much it is necessary to trust the quality of spare parts and tools that are used on board, which is why we have partnerships with local and nationwide suppliers. Dealing only with renowned, certified and homologated brands.

Fresh provisions and bonded store

Top quality at reasonable prices for anything that your crew needs on their voyage.
Official and internal inspections are carried out to ensure food safety, inventory rotation, temperature control and hygiene, from the selection of products, from renowned national and international brands, to arrival on the ship, in refrigerated trucks with monitored temperature.

Nautical Charts, publications & flags

Complete nautical chart, publications and flags coverage to help vessels navigate as per SOLAS regulation.

Chemicals, marine paint and gases

We offer the most comprehensive marine chemicals and gases available by combining reliable services and competitive pricing with a global support network.


Lifeboat/raft inspection, among others

Regular lifeboat inspection and maintenance will ensure your equipment remains in a state of readiness.
We provide certified technicians authorised to carry out periodic inspection and testing of lifeboats and rafts to ensure the safety of the crew during service.
